The Pizookie Recipe:

The pizzokie is a delicous desert item that is suprisingly easy to make. With it's icecream cover and cookie chunk bottom; it combines the soft and chunky textures to produce an immaculate taste. This is a staple desert found in the restuarant BJ's, with many different variations coming from it's main cookie chunk and flavoring. Following this recipe you will learn how to make a simple Choclate Chip Pizookie!
