The Pizookie Recipe:

The pizookie is a delicous desert item that is suprisingly easy to make. With it's icecream cover and cookie chunk bottom; it combines the soft and chunky textures to produce an immaculate taste. This is a staple desert found in the restuarant BJ's, with many different variations coming from it's main cookie chunk and flavoring. Following this recipe you will learn how to make a simple Choclate Chip Pizookie!

Ingredients:

For 12 servings
  • All Purpose Flour - 1 1/2 Cups
  • Rolled Oats - 1/2 Cups
  • Baking Soda - 1/2 Teaspoons
  • Ground Cinnamon - 1/2 Teaspoons
  • Salt - 1/2 Teaspooons
  • White Sugar - 1/2 Cups + 2 Teaspoons
  • Softened Butter - 3/4 Cups
  • Packed Light Brown Sugar - 1/2 Cups
  • Large Eggs - 2
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 Teaspooon
  • Chcolate Chips - 12 Ounces
  • Chopped Pecans - 1/2 Cups
  • Icecream - 2 Scoops

Steps:

  1. Preheat your ovten to 350 Degrees F.
  2. Whisk the flour, oats, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt all together.
  3. Beat the white sugar, butter, brown sugar, and cream until the texture becomes light and fluflly for 3-5 minutes. After, add eggs one at a time and mix until the eggs are fully integrated. Add the vanilla extract in, and keep mixing until everything is combined. Dump the dry ingredients from the previous step in and continue mixing
  4. Add choclate chips and pecans.
  5. Tranfer the dough to a iron skillet and distribute your the dough over the bottom of the skillet.
  6. Bake the dough in a pre-heated oven the top is firm and the edges start to pull inward; takes around 35 - 40 minutes.
  7. Take it out the oven and let it cool for 10 - 15 minutes. Using a spatula unstick the sides of a cookie and put the cookie onto a cutting board.
  8. Add 2 scoops of your icecream to the top of the coookie

And now your Pizookie should be all ready to eat!
