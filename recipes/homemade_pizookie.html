The Pizookie Recipe:

The pizzokie is a delicous desert item that is suprisingly easy to make. With it's icecream cover and cookie chunk bottom; it combines the soft and chunky textures to produce an immaculate taste. This is a staple desert found in the restuarant BJ's, with many different variations coming from it's main cookie chunk and flavoring. Following this recipe you will learn how to make a simple Choclate Chip Pizookie!

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour - 1 1/2 Cups
  • Rolled Oats - 1/2 Cups
  • Baking Soda - 1/2 Teaspoons
  • Ground Cinnamon - 1/2 Teaspoons
  • Salt - 1/2 Teaspooons
  • Salt - 1/2 Teaspoons
  • White Sugar - 1/2 Cups + 2 Teaspoons
  • Softened Butter - 3/4 Cups
  • Packed Light Brown Sugar - 1/2 Cups
  • Large Eggs - 2
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 Teaspooon
  • Chcolate Chips - 12 Ounces
  • Chopped Pecans - 1/2 Cups

Steps

  1. Preheat your ovten to 350 Degrees F
  2. Whisk the flour, oats, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt all together
  3. Mix together white sugar, butter, brown sugar, cream until the texture becomes light and fluflly for 3-5 minutes. After, add eggs one at a time and mix until the eggs are fully mixed in. Mix some vanilla extract in, and keep mixing until everything is combined. Add
  4. Add choclate chips and pecans
  5. Tranfer the dough to a iron skillet and distribute your fingers over the bottom of the skillet.
  6. Bake the dough in a pre-heated oven the top is firm and the edges start to pull inward; takes around 35 - 40 minutes
  7. Take it out the oven and let it cool for 10 - 15 minutes. Using a spatula unstick the sides of a cookie and put the cookie onto a cutting board

And now your Pizookie should be all ready to eat!
