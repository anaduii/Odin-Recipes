The Cream and Crab Sushi Recipe:

Sushi is a very common dish in Japan and it's popularity has now spread all over the world. It's a very simple seafood item to make with it's main ingredients being a wrapping of rice and seaweed around it's core meat. In this guide, you will learn how to make an unique variation of sushi, Cream and Crab Cheese.

Ingredients:

For 2 servings
  • Uncooked White Rice - 1 Cup
  • Water - 2 Cups
  • Rice Vinegar - 2 Tablespoons
  • Salt - 1 Teaspoon
  • Nori Seaweed Sheets - 2 Sheets
  • Peeled cucumber sliced lengthwise - 1/4
  • Imitation crab legs - 2 pieces
  • Cream cheese sliced - 1/2 of a 3 ounce package
  • Minced fresh ginger root - 1 Teaspoon

Steps:

  1. Boil rice and water in a pan in high heat. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cover until the rice is tender.
  2. After the liquid becomes cool in 20 - 25 minutes, stir the rice in vinegar and salt; allow it completley cool.
  3. Moisturize your hands with water and then lay out the seaweed sheets. Spread rice on all the sheets, leaving a 1/2 inch gap on each edge.
  4. Add cucumber, imitation crab meat and cream cheese strips on top of the rice opposite to the gap. Roll up the sushi from the toppings side until the sushi is fully rolled.
  5. Using a knife, cut the sushi roll in 5-6 pieces and then serve with minced ginger.

Get ready to eat your Cream and Crab Sushi!
