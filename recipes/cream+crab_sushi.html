The Cream and Crab Sushi

Sushi is a very common dish in Japan and it's popularity has now spread all over the world. It's a very simple seafood item to make with it's main ingredients being a wrapping of rice and seaweed around it's core item. In this guide, you will learn how to make an unique variation of sushi, Cream and Crab Cheese.

Ingredients:

For 2 servings
  • Uncooked White Rice - 1 Cup
  • Water - 2 Cups
  • Rice Vinegar - 2 Tablespoons
  • Salt - 1 Teaspoon
  • Nori Seaweed Sheets - 2 Sheets
  • Peeled cucumber sliced lengthwise - 1/4
  • Imitation crab legs - 2 pieces
  • Cream cheese sliced - 1/2 of a 3 ounce package
  • Minced fresh ginger root - 1 Teaspoon
