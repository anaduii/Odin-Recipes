The Basic Tuna Sandwhich Recipe:

If you are ever hungry and don't have the time to cook, make this quick and easy sandwhich. All you need it tuna and sandwhich ingredients, nothing fancy is required. This is the perfect healthy snack, but if it is too small, you can always amp up the bread and have a full meal.

Ingredients:

  • Wheat Bread - 1/4 of a footlong
  • Black Pepper - 1 Teaspoon
  • Mayonaisse - 2 Tablespoon
  • Jalapenos - 6 to 10 pieces
  • Tuna Container - 1 Tablespoon
  • Lettuce - 0.5 Gram

Steps:

  2. Using a can opener, open the tuna can and pour all the liquid out. Then put it in a bowl
  3. With a spoon, get the Mayonaisse out of its jar and put 2 table spoons of mayo on the tuna in the bowl.
  4. Mix the tuna and mayonaisse for about a minute, until the main is one with the tuna; add black pepper to finish the combo
  5. Cut the bread into 2 slices and spread the tuna and mayonaisse combo onto the bread
  6. Finally, add the lettuce pieces on top of the tuna and spread out the jalapenos

The Basic Tuna Sandwhich is now ready to eat!
