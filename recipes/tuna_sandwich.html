The Basic Tuna Sandwhich Recipe:

If you are ever hungry and don't have the time to cook, make this quick and easy sandwhich. All you need is tuna, mayo and some bread; nothing fancy is required. This is a perfectly healthy snack by itself, but if it is too bland you can add your own sanwich ingredients like I have.

Ingredients:

For 2 servings
  • Any Bread - 2 Slices
  • Black Pepper - 1 Teaspoon
  • Mayonaisse - 2 Tablespoon
  • Jalapenos - 6 to 10 pieces
  • Tuna Container - 1 Tablespoon
  • Lettuce - 0.5 Gram

Steps:

  1. Using a can opener, open the tuna can and pour all the liquid out. Then place the tuna in a bowl.
  2. With a spoon, get the mayonaisse out of its jar and put 2 table spoons of mayo on the tuna in the bowl.
  3. Mix the tuna and mayonaisse for about a minute, until they are both blended together; add black pepper at the end.
  4. Spread the combination of tuna and mayonaisse on both slices of the bread
  5. Finally, add lettuce pieces on top of the tuna and spread out the jalapenos

The Basic Tuna Sandwhich is now ready to eat!
